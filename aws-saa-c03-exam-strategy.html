AWS SAA-C03 Exam Strategy: What I Wish I Had Known

Preparing for the AWS Certified Solutions Architect Associate (SAA-C03) exam can feel overwhelming. With so many services, scenarios, and study materials, it’s easy to get lost. Here’s what I wish I had known before starting my journey — including my strategy, mistakes, and the exact tools I used.

Understand the Exam First

The SAA-C03 exam isn’t just about memorizing services — it’s about designing secure, resilient, high-performing, and cost-optimized architectures.

  • 65 questions (50 scored, 15 unscored)
  • 130 minutes
  • Passing score: 720/1000
  • Domains:
    • Design Secure Architectures (30%)
    • Design Resilient Architectures (26%)
    • Design High-Performing Architectures (24%)
    • Design Cost-Optimized Architectures (20%)

❌ Mistakes I Made (and Fixed)

Mistake 1: Trying to learn everything at once
✅ Fix: I created a Google Sheet Study Stack with topic breakdowns, flashcards, and a “To-Do” tracker.
Mistake 2: Delaying practice questions
✅ Fix: I started using free questions early to reinforce concepts and understand AWS’s scenario framing.

Free Resources

  • AWS Skill Builder: Approved ways to learn directly from AWS, including interactive labs and learning paths.
  • AWS Whitepapers: Especially the Well-Architected Framework, which is essential for understanding exam scenarios.
  • Coding N Concepts: Provides a fantastic breakdown and analysis of AWS SAA-C03 notes and key topics.
  • Practice Questions: Prepare effectively by solving AWS Solutions Architect Associate exam questions that closely follow the real SAA-C03 format and cover all important AWS services and architectural concepts.
Mistake 3: Ignoring architecture diagrams
✅ Fix: I added visuals for VPC, ELB, Auto Scaling, and Route 53 to my study stack.

My Winning Strategy

  • Read the official exam guide and understand the domains
  • Use my Google Sheet Study Stack to stay organized
  • Mix flashcards, whitepapers, and videos
  • Start practice questions early
  • Review architecture diagrams weekly
  • Simulate exam conditions with timed sets

Why This Certification Matters

The SAA-C03 is one of the most in-demand certifications globally. It opens doors to roles like:

  • Cloud Solutions Architect
  • DevOps Engineer
  • Cloud Consultant
  • Infrastructure Engineer

Whether you're freelancing, job hunting, or building your authority stack — this cert is a game-changer.
