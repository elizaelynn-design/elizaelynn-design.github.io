Smart Exam Strategy

  • Master AWS Monitoring Tools: CloudWatch, CloudTrail, and AWS Config.
  • Practice Automation: Use CloudFormation and Systems Manager for infrastructure as code.
  • Focus on Security: Understand IAM roles, policies, and best practices.
  • Hands-On Labs: Gain experience with EC2, S3, VPC, and CLI operations.
  • Practice Exams: Take at least two full-length practice tests to gauge your readiness.
