AWS Certified AI Practitioner (AIF C01) – Your Complete Guide to Exam Success

The cloud industry is evolving fast and artificial intelligence (AI) is leading the charge. If you're looking to validate your foundational knowledge of AI and machine learning (ML) within the AWS ecosystem, the AWS Certified AI Practitioner certification is a powerful first step

What Is the AWS Certified AI Practitioner Exam?

The AWS Certified AI Practitioner exam (code: AIF C01) tests your grasp of:

  • Core AI and ML concepts
  • Generative AI models and use cases
  • AWS services like SageMaker, Rekognition, and Comprehend
  • Ethical considerations in AI including bias, fairness, and responsible use

Key Topics You Will Be Tested On

  • AI Fundamentals – Algorithms, supervised and unsupervised learning, data types
  • Generative AI – Text generation, image synthesis, and model types
  • AWS AI Services – SageMaker, Lex, Polly, Comprehend, and more
  • Ethics and Governance – Bias detection, fairness, responsible AI practices
